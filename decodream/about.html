About Decodream

Welcome to Decodream! We’re on a mission to transform spaces into unique expressions of style, comfort, and functionality. At Decodream, we believe in the power of design to inspire, uplift, and bring dreams to life through thoughtful interiors that speak to our clients' tastes and lifestyles.

How to Use Decodream

  • Upload your 2D blueprint or layout, and we’ll generate a 3D model that you can explore.
  • Use our platform’s customization tools to adjust layouts, materials, colors, and lighting in real-time. Our design team is available for feedback and revisions until you’re satisfied with the final look.
  • If you have questions or need guidance, check out our chatbot. Our bot is here to help you through each step, answer any questions, and ensure your design experience is smooth and enjoyable.
